My Portofolio

Fired from one career path, I redirected my passion for robotics into a venture that not only satisfied enthusiasts but also added an element of excitement to the tech world. The shift from employee to entrepreneur showcased resilience and adaptability, turning an unexpected exit into a victorious entrance into the world of robotic combat. Today, BrawlBots Arena stands as a testament to transforming challenges into thrilling opportunities.
